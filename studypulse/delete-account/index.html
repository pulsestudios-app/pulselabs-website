StudyPulse account deletion

Delete Your StudyPulse Account

We respect your right to delete your account and personal data. This page explains how to request account deletion for StudyPulse.

What Gets Deleted

  • Account information, including email and display name
  • All generated study materials, transcripts, summaries, quizzes, flashcards, and mind maps
  • Saved community content
  • Subscription information stored by StudyPulse
  • Usage history and preferences
  • Any reports or blocks you've created

What Might Be Retained

Some data may be retained for legal compliance:

  • Payment records required by tax law, retained for up to 7 years
  • Anonymized analytics data that no longer identifies your account
  • Logs required for security, abuse prevention, or fraud prevention

How To Request Deletion

Option A - Email Request

Send an email to support@pulsestudios.app with:

  • Subject: "Account Deletion Request - StudyPulse"
  • Your account email address
  • Confirmation you want to delete your account

We will process your request within 30 days.

Option B - Web Form

Submitting opens a prefilled email to support@pulsestudios.app. We do not store form entries on this page.

Request received. We'll process within 30 days and send confirmation to your email.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are processed within 30 days as required by Google Play Store policy. You'll receive a confirmation email when complete.

Cancel Subscriptions First

Before deleting, please cancel any active subscriptions through Google Play Store to avoid future charges. Account deletion does not automatically cancel subscriptions.

Manage Google Play subscriptions

Subscription Cancellation Instructions

  1. Open Google Play Store on your device
  2. Tap profile icon → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions
  3. Tap StudyPulse
  4. Tap "Cancel subscription"
